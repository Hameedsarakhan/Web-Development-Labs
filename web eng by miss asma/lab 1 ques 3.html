Story Completion Guideline

In order to send your story for the monthly horror story contest,you have to follow the guidelines below.


Stories can have the following Categories.

  1. Novel
  2. Short Story
  3. Real life Story
  4. Articles

Here are the writing guidelines

  1. Basic Guidelines
    1. Story must be within 1200 words
    2. Computer Typed:
      1. Font size:12 pt
      2. Paragraph: Double line break
      3. Font name: Times New Roman
    3. Well formatted with a front page
    4. Front page content:
      1. Story Name
      2. Author Name
      3. Submission Date
      4. Category
